For my Lab 2 map, I am planning to create a "Climate State of the Union" chloropleth map. It will take data from Google Earth Engine averaged over the calendar year 2025 in the following attributes

Mean Annual Temp (ERA5-Land): The average air temperature (converted from Kelvin to Celsius).

Total Annual Precip (CHIRPS): The cumulative rainfall in millimeters.

Annual Median NDVI (MODIS): A measure of vegetation health, using the median to avoid cloud-contaminated outliers.

Total Burned Area (MODIS): Calculated by summing the area of all pixels flagged as "burned" in 2025.

Population Density (GPW v4): Using the most recent high-resolution UN-adjusted count.

50 enumeration areas, 5 attributes
