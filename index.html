Climate State of the Union

2025 Environmental Metrics

This interactive dashboard visualizes key environmental and climate variables across the United States for the year 2025. To ensure a fair comparison between states of vastly different sizes and populations, the data has been normalized. Rather than showing raw totals, metrics like burned area are shown as a percentage of total state land area, and population is displayed as density per square mile. Climate variables such as temperature and precipitation have been converted to Standard units (Fahrenheit and Inches) for easier interpretation.

Data Source: Processed via Google Earth Engine using ERA5-Land, CHIRPS, MODIS, and GPWv411 datasets.
